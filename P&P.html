Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how P.E.S. Canteen handles information when you use our ordering system.

Information We Collect

We only collect order-related information (such as items ordered, table number, order code, and transaction details). We do not collect personal information like phone numbers, email addresses, or names.

Use of Data

The data we collect is used solely for processing and managing food orders, inventory tracking, and canteen operations.

Data Storage

Order history is securely stored in our database (Firebase). No sensitive personal data is stored.

Payments

Payments are processed securely via Razorpay. We do not store any card or payment details on our systems.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share customer data with any third parties except as required by law or for payment processing.

Contact

If you have any concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out to the canteen administration at P.E.S. College of Engineering, Nagsenvana, Chh. Sambhajinagar, Maharashtra - 431001.

Return & Refund Policy

This policy applies to all purchases made at P.E.S. Canteen.

No Return or Refund

As food and beverages are prepared fresh to order, we do not allow cancellations, returns, or refunds once the order has been placed and payment has been confirmed.

Exceptions

  • If your payment was deducted but the order was not generated due to a technical error, we will verify the transaction and initiate a refund through Razorpay within 7 working days.
  • Refunds, if approved, will be processed back to the original payment method.
